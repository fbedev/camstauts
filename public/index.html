What motuscam does

Designed for crews who want one place to see how every capture device is performing without extra clutter.

Mirror mode

Flip the preview instantly so talent can check posture, eye line, and marker placement before a take.

Session tracking

Automatically log recording time, number of clips, and devices in use. Everyone sees the same totals instantly.

Simple sync

Connect multiple devices and keep the same project stats everywhere. motuscam pulls a global status board for you.

How it fits on set

Three quick steps to keep everyone aligned.

  1. 1

    Open motuscam and pick your project

    Use the preset templates or start a fresh scene. Everything you log stays attached to that session.

  2. 2

    Switch to mirror or standard view

    Tap the mirror toggle so talent can self-check movements while the rest of the crew watches the shared status panel.

  3. 3

    Track the takes you keep

    Mark clips as keepers and motuscam updates the global board so every device mirrors the same totals.
